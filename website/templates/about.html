About us

Tasco metal construction limited registered with RC400437. It is an indigenous multidiscipline firm incorporated in Nigeria since 19th January 2001 to render professional services in the areas of civil engineering, steel Structures, fabrications, building services and products supplies. The company has been involved with difference of clients both in the public and private sectors and collaborated as a sub-contractor with some other leading firms in building Contracting and Consulting engineer Firm in the country.
